This modification adds the ability to create 5 weapon stores to the game.
In weapon shops, you can buy almost all the weapons that are in the game, with the exception of melee weapons (there is only a knife and brass knuckles), tear grenades and a fire extinguisher.

Cheat codes:
GUNN - create a weapon shop
DEL - delete the shop (you need to get on the store label)
CLRW - delete all shops
Keys:
X - view the store number (you need to stand on the store label)

In order to create a shop you need to enter the GUNN cheat code, stand in the right place and press one of the keys from 1 to 5. Then follow the prompts on the screen. After you create stores, restart DYOM, then icons and store labels will appear on the map.

The main gameplay feature of the stores is that they will work even in cutscenes. That is, it will be possible to teleport the player to the store at the right moment, add a cutscene for 99999 seconds and teleport the player back. So the player will be able to choose the right weapon, skip the cutscene and continue the game. Thanks to this feature, stores can be used both in linear "corridor" missions and in missions with a conditional "open world".

All store coordinates and settings are stored in the "Weapon shops.ini" file.
